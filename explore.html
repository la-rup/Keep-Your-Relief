SUPPORT

The National Alliance on Mental Illnesses offers a COVID-19 Guide that offers a range of information from manging anxiety, accessing medical care, and dealing with loss. Their mission is to "provide advocacy, education, support and public awareness so that all individuals and families affected by mental illness can build better lives."

The USA government website contains a COVID-19 support page that highlights the various types of federal government assistance offered to individuals and businesses affected by the pandemic, as well as health information about the virus itself.

Feeding America is a nonprofit organization dedicated to "advance change in America by ensuring equitable access to nutritious food for all in partnership with food banks, policymakers, supporters, and the communities [they] serve." They offer COVID relief information on locations to access free food, assistance from charitable programs, and support links to sign up for state and federal food programs.
